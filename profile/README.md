# Hello World, we are VAIT! 👋

**VAIT** - **Vietnamese Aussies in I.T.** is a local community of I.T. Enthusiasts working
and living in Australia. Our HQ is in Melbourne, VIC, but we welcome all brothers and sisters
across Australia and all around the world!
